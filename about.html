About The Couple

About Taylor

Taylor was born outside of Dallas, Texas and moved to Leesburg, VA when he was 13 years old. He has two brothers, Zachary and Mitchell.

Growing up, Taylor was a proficient Chess Player and Lego builder. When he was 12, he built is first website in HTML.

He attended The University of Virginia and received a dual degree in Classics and Math. At UVA, Taylor became a member of Alpha Tao Omega, where he met his good friend Jaryd Waegerle and his three roommates, Ian, Greg, and Aaron. Taylor was also a member of UVA’s Campus Crusade for Christ chapter, Agape. Through Agape, he met Susan Carrai, who would later become one of Emily’s good friends and introduce them to each other.

Taylor currently lives in Arlington, VA with this three roommates and works in Web Design and Development for Interactive Strategies, a Washington D.C. based design and marketing firm.

His hobbies include making dinner for Emily, website design (this one for example), and going on hikes with Emily.

Fun fact: one of the first things Taylor noticed about Emily was the Vineyard Vines whale on the back of her car.

About Emily

Emily was born in Baltimore, Maryland, but grew up in Rochester and then Albany, New York. She has two older siblings, Allison and Tim.

She was destined for Washington D.C. from childhood. As a little girl she would often pretend to be the Press Secretary, presenting eloquent speeches behind her podium/dresser to the most influential of stuffed animal reporters.

She attended Providence College in Rhode Island, where she received a B.A. in Political Marketing. After moving to DC in the Fall of 2012, she put her education to good use and currently works as an account manager at the news publication POLITICO, managing branding and advertising campaigns.

Some of Emily's hobbies are serving in the Children's ministry at her church, Portico Arlington, being outdoors, and letting Taylor make dinner.

Emily lives in Arlington, VA with her roommate Sarah.

The Proposal

How he proposed

To fully understand how Taylor proposed to Emily, we must go back to before they started dating and before they even went on their first date...

One Sunday after church in Fall 2013, Taylor went to a bar to watch a 49ers game with Emily and her roommate Sarah. Coincidentally, he had just finished making his first homebrewed beer, so he made sure to bring bottles for Emily and Sarah to try. Emily took the beer home with her and thought it was the best beer she had ever had and would ever have in her entire life. Although her friends laughed at her, she saved the beer bottle and tucked it away in her dresser because she knew there was something special about Taylor. Once she and Taylor officially started dating, the bottle sat on her dresser with a pink rose in it. Knowing this story, Taylor made sure to incorporate it into the proposal.

The proposal happened on January 19th on the Georgetown Waterfront, where Taylor and Emily had their first date. They went to Baked and Wired, a cupcake store in Georgetown, and then walked down to the Waterfront. As they walked along the water, they arrived at an overlook where Taylor had asked his Best Man, Jaryd, to arrange twelve pink roses in beer bottles that were identical to the original bottle. Each rose had a tag around it with a reason Taylor loves Emily and wants to marry her, along with a corresponding Bible verse.

Taylor’s best man Jaryd and his fiancé Laura hid in the bushes to capture this special moment.

Needless to say, Emily said “yes”.
